hmmrd quick play is the fast moving party version of the cult hit drinking game hmmrd!

Number of Players

4 to 10

Setup

Have each player pour 4 drinks of equal size of whatever they'd like to drink. Everyone drinks 2 of them and puts the other 2 in the center of the table. Fill up a single King's Cup to the brim and place it in the center of the table. This cup will be drunk last.

Gameplay

Players take turns in a clockwise order. On your turn choose people to play with/against as instructed by the card on the top of the deck. Once teams are assembled, flip the card over.

You must attempt to complete the task written on the card. If you succeed, you may give away a number of drinks as indicated on the 'success' part of the card. If you fail, you must take a number of drinks as indicated on the 'failure' part of the card. For team challenges, each player the losing team counts as having failed and must drink. Each player on the winning team counts as having succeeded and may give drinks away. Drinks are taken from the cups in the center of the table. Some cards cannot be won/lost and are just a rule you must follow. These are called "Rules Cards". After performing the task the game proceeds to the next player. If you cannot complete the task, you are out of the game.

When only the King's Cup is left in the center of the table, the game proceeds to sudden death mode. In this mode, you will only drink if you lose a card on the turn that you drew it. If you draw a card and win, you do not get to give a drink away, and if you lose a team challenge (when you did not draw the card), you are not forced to drink. If the person drawing the card does not lose, the game proceeds to the next player. When the King's Cup is drunk, the game is over.
